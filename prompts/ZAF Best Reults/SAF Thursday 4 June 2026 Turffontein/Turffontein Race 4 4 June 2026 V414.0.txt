  - The Issue: Late Bloomer's previous races were at Kenilworth and Durbanville

  - The Failure Mode: A standard Maiden Plate in the Western Cape features a

  - The Calibration: We integrated a regional gravity shift modifier

    \text{If Circuit}_{\text{prior}} \in \{\text{Kenilworth}, \text{Durbanville}\} \land \text{Circuit}_{\text{current}} \in \{\text{Turffontein}, \text{Vaal}\}, \text{apply } \Delta \text{Gravity} = +2.0\text{ to LPP}_i

Gap C: Binary Layoff Penalisation

  - The Issue: 144.1 applied a strict, linear penalty to any horse returning
    from a layoff of over 90 days.
  - The Failure Mode: Late Bloomer had been off for 142 days. A standard penalty
    would have added +3.0 to her \text{PVI}, dropping her \text{NKE} to 60.22
    and flagging her as a high-risk runner.
  - The Calibration: We introduced an override gate using the Jockey/Stable
    Transfer Index (\text{JIFM}). When a horse transfers to a highveld stable
    with private training tracks (such as Tony Peter's), the training regime
    compensates for the lack of recent races. If \text{JIFM} \ge 0.35, the
    layoff penalty is set to 0.00.
