    centrifugal drag penalty, reducing their kinetic energy reserves before they
    reach the straight.

3. Winter Inside Rail Degradation Decay (WIRDD)

This formula automatically scales down the Dry Compaction Inside Rail Bonus
(DCHL) during winter racing:
\Delta\nu_{\text{DCHL\_adjusted}} = \Delta\nu_{\text{DCHL}} \cdot \left(1.0 - \beta_{\text{winter}} \cdot I_{\text{winter}} \cdot \left(1.0 - \frac{\text{Rail\_Position}}{10}\right)\right)
Where:

  - \beta_{\text{winter}} = 1.00 (complete suppression factor)
  - I_{\text{winter}} = 1.00 (binary indicator for the winter season)
  - This formula suppresses the inside compaction bonus, preventing the model
    from overestimating low-drawn runners when the inside rail is degraded.
