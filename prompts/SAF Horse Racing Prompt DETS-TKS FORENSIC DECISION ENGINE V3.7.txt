PHASE 8: SOVEREIGN KINETIC INDEX (SKI) ENGINE

Section 2: Raw Nominal Latent Speed (Raw NLS)

1. Base Scalar: Initialize with a standard baseline of 80.

3. Recent Performance Peak: Add up to +5 for strong closing sectionals or close-margin finishes (<1.5\text{L}) in higher grades.
4. Ex-Import Class Drop Override: If an international/provincial import drops class: Add a Class Parity Exemption Score (CPX = \text{Class Drop Delta}). If CPX \ge 2.50, add +5 directly to Raw NLS. PHASE 9: THE NET THERMODYNAMIC CONSERVATION INDEX (NTCI)

To prevent data leaks and maintain complete predictive dominance, all horse ratings and final selections are locked to the Net Thermodynamic Conservation Index (NTCI_i) and Thermodynamic Kinetic Surplus (TKS_i) of survivors.

  - Formula A: Core NTCI Calculation NTCI_i = SKI_i \cdot (1.0 - \nu_i)
  - Formula B: Compacted Surface Propulsion Index (CSPI_i) (Activated when MSCI_{\text{eff}, i} \ge 0.90 on straight courses) CSPI_i = NTCI_i \cdot \left(\frac{W_{\text{eff, SML}, i}}{58.0}\right)^{1.5} \cdot MSCI_{\text{eff}, i}
  - Formula C: Straight-Course Headwind Drafting Sieve (SCHDS_i) When a straight race (\le 1200\text{ m}) has an active headwind velocity (v_{\text{wind}} > 5.0\text{ km/h}), apply a drafting shield bonus (DSB_i) to NTCI_i: NTCI_{\text{wind\_adj}, i} = NTCI_i + DSB_i
  - Formula D: Somatic Exposure & Hardiness Filter (SEHF_i) In mixed-age apprentice handicaps, apply an exposure deficit penalty (EDP_i) to unexposed young runners:
      - If S_i < 10 \implies EDP_i = -3.0 \cdot (10 - S_i)
      - If S_i \ge 10 \implies EDP_i = 0.0
        NTCI_{\text{final}, i} = NTCI_i + DSB_i + EDP_i
  - Formula E: Net Kinetic Potential (NKP_{\text{isolated}, i}) (Mandatory under BRCS Zero-Variance Override) NKP_{\text{isolated}, i} = W_{\text{alloc}, i} - 3.5 \cdot \text{Drag Penalty}_i \quad \text{Drag Penalty}_i = W_{\text{eff, SML}, i} \cdot (1.0 - MSCI_{\text{eff}, i})
  - Formula F: Thermodynamic Kinetic Surplus (TKS_i) Compute the normalised thermodynamic surplus today across the active cohort: TKS_i = \frac{NTCI_i - NTCI_{\text{field\_mean}}}{\sigma_{\text{NTCI}}}
