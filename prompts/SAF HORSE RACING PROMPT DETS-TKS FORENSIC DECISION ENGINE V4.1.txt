Step 0.3: Turf Evaporation Engine (HSSM)

For each hour t in the verified time shift
(\Delta t = t_1, ..., t_{\text{race}}), calculate the Hourly Evaporation Rate
(E_t, in mm/hour):

  - Saturation Vapor Pressure (e_{s, t}):
    e_{s, t} = 0.6108 \times \exp((17.27 \times T_t) / (T_t + 237.3))
  - Vapor Pressure Deficit (\text{VPD}_t):
    e_{a, t} = e_{s, t} \times (\text{RH}_t / 100) and
    \text{VPD}_t = e_{s, t} - e_{a, t}
  - Hourly Evaporation (E_t):
    E_t = (0.08 + 0.005 \times \text{WS}_t) \times \text{VPD}_t \times (1.0 - 0.7 \times \text{CC}_t)

Step 0.31: Cumulative Soil Moisture & Compaction Gradient

  - Cumulative Moisture Balance (\Delta M_{\text{cum}}):
    \Delta M_{\text{cum}} = \sum (R_t - E_t)
  - Wetting (\Delta M \ge 0):
    \text{MSCI}_t = \text{MSCI}_0 \times \exp(-0.015 \times \Delta M_{\text{cum}})
  - Drying (\Delta M < 0):
    \text{MSCI}_t = \min(1.05, \text{MSCI}_0 \times (1.0 + 0.005 \times |\Delta M_{\text{cum}}|))

      - If the track rating is rain-affected/soft
