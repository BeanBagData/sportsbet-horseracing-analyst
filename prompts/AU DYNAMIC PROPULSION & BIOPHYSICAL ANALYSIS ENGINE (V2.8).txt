\mathbb{I}_{\text{Scratched}, k} is a binary indicator that equals 1 if the

\text{SDI}_i = |S_i - B_{\text{actual}, i}|

\text{PVV}_i = |\text{AF5}_i - \text{CAF}_i| Where \text{AF5}_i is the average

6.1 S-Curve Dynamic Soil Fluidisation (E_{\text{rheology}, i}) & Stefan Viscous
Adhesion Modulator (\text{SVAC}_i)

To calculate compaction on clay-loam turf, execute the Cumulative
Mass-Displacement Index (\text{CMDI}_r):
\text{CMDI}_r = \sum_{k=1}^{r-1} \sum_{j=1}^{N_{\text{active}, k}} \left( W_{\text{eff}, j, k} \times \mathbb{I}(\text{Lane}_{j, k} \le 2) \right)
Where W_{\text{eff}, j, k} is the effective weight of runner j in previous race
k. The S-Curve Soil Fluidisation Multiplier (\text{SCSFM}_r) calculates the
transition of clay-loam soil from elastic to plastic fluid states:
\text{SCSFM}_r = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-k_{\text{fluid}} \cdot (\text{CMDI}_r - \text{CMDI}_{\text{crit}})}} \times \theta_{\text{moisture}}^2 \quad \left( \text{CMDI}_{\text{crit}} = 5500\text{ kg}, \ k_{\text{fluid}} = 0.001 \right)
The kinetic energy loss per stride is computed as:
E_{\text{rheology}, i} = C_{\text{soil}} \cdot \left[ \frac{W_{\text{eff}, i}}{A_{\text{hoof}}} \right]^{\frac{1}{n_{\text{soil}}}} \cdot \left( \theta_{\text{moisture}} \cdot [1.0 + \text{SCSFM}_r] \right)^2
Where:

  - E_{\text{rheology}, i} is the total kinetic energy loss per stride (Joules).
  - C_{\text{soil}} is the soil deformation constant (clay loam
    \= 3.14 \times 10^3; Pakenham Polytrack = 1.05 \times 10^2).
  - A_{\text{hoof}} is the hoof contact area (1.15 \times 10^{-2}\text{ m}^2).
  - n_{\text{soil}} is the soil compaction exponent. On Heavy 8+ turf,
    n_{\text{soil}} = 0.65. On Good 4 turf, n_{\text{soil}} = 1.25.
  - \theta_{\text{moisture}} is the moisture saturation fraction (Heavy 8
    \= 0.85; Good 4 = 0.15).

The viscous suction drag created by the wet boundary layer is calculated using
Stefan's Law of Viscous Adhesion. Stefan's Law Cadence Modulator (\text{SLCM}_i)
models hoof contact time (\Delta t_{c, i}):
\text{SLCM}_i = \left( \frac{\Delta t_{\text{benchmark}}}{\Delta t_{c, i}} \right)^2 \quad \left( \Delta t_{\text{benchmark}} = 0.12\text{ s} \right)
If high-cadence telemetry is unavailable, calculate \text{SLCM}_i from stride
frequency (SF_i in Hertz): \text{SLCM}_i = \left( \frac{SF_i}{2.25} \right)^4.
The Stefan Viscous Adhesion Coefficient (\text{SVAC}_i) is adjusted by the
cadence modulator:
\text{SVAC}_i = \frac{\kappa \cdot \left( W_{\text{eff}, i} \cdot \theta_{\text{moisture}} \right)^3}{\text{SLCM}_i} \quad \left( \kappa = 1.42 \times 10^{-4} \right)

to prevent probability explosion:
P(\text{horse}_i) = \frac{\exp\left( \frac{U_i^* - \overline{U}^*}{\tau} \right)}{\sum_{j=1}^{N_{\text{active}}} \exp\left( \frac{U_j^* - \overline{U}^*}{\tau} \right)} \quad (\tau = 4.0\text{ NLS})
Where
\overline{U}^* = \frac{1}{N_{\text{active}}} \sum_{j=1}^{N_{\text{active}}} U_j^*
is the field mean unified utility, and f_i = \ln(P(\text{horse}_i)).

8.2 Fundamental Biophysical Utility Calibration The probability vector
P(\text{horse}_i) is constructed purely from fundamental kinetic utilities
without market odds input or external betting noise. This provides an
uncontaminated biophysical probability baseline for winner isolation.

8.3 Goodness-of-Fit Metric: Pseudo-R^2 (\eta^2) Evaluate the genuine biophysical
win-informational value of your model relative to an uninformative baseline:
\eta^2(\text{model}) = 1 - \frac{LL(\text{Model})}{LL(\text{Random})} Where
LL(\text{Model}) is the log-likelihood of the fundamental biophysical
probability model: LL = \sum_{r=1}^{M} \ln(P(\text{winner}_r))

  - Programmatic Mandate: If \eta^2 \ge 0.05, the model contains significant,
    unique biophysical predictive value. The race is classified as Edge Class A
    (Low Entropy, High Conversion). If \eta^2 < 0.01, downgrade the race to Edge
    Class E and abstain from win staking.

8.4 Single-Outcome Win Optimisation (Single Kelly) Calculate the optimal win
stake fraction (f^*) directly for the single selected winner (s_1) using its
fundamental biophysical edge:
f^* = \left( P(\text{horse}_{s_1}) - \frac{1 - P(\text{horse}_{s_1})}{\text{Implied Kinetic Yield}_{s_1}} \right) \times \text{Multiplier}
Where the Multiplier is scaled dynamically by Edge Class: Class A = 0.20
(fractional Kelly), Class B = 0.15, Class C = 0.10, Class D = 0.05, Class E
= 0.00 (abstain). All hedging, split staking, and coverage allocations are
deactivated.
