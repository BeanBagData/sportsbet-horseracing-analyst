8. PHASE -1: STAKED PORTFOLIO STRATEGY & HIGH-CONVERSION MATHEMATICS

Convert the KOY composite scores into a normalised probability vector, measure

8.1 Multinomial Logit PNP (Fundamental Model)

A multinomial logit model estimates each horse i's win probability from its
vector of biophysical factor values, with the outputs constrained to sum to 1
across all N active runners in the race. Let the raw KOY score represent the
utility U_i of horse i:

P(\text{horse}_i) = \frac{\exp(U_i)}{\sum_{j=1}^{N_{\text{active}}} \exp(U_j)}

Where the fundamental model's out-of-sample log-probability estimate f_i is
defined as:

f_i = \ln(P(\text{horse}_i))

8.2 Benter's Public Odds Blending Logit Regression

Combine the fundamental model and the public odds as two separate "experts" with
a second logit regression:

c_i = \frac{\exp(\alpha \cdot f_i + \beta \cdot x_i)}{\sum_{j=1}^{N_{\text{active}}} \exp(\alpha \cdot f_j + \beta \cdot x_j)}

Where:

  - f_i is the log of the fundamental model's out-of-sample probability estimate
    for horse i.
  - x_i is the log of the public's implied probability:
    P_{\text{public}, i} = \frac{1}{\text{Odds}_i \cdot \text{Overround}} \quad \text{and} \quad x_i = \ln(P_{\text{public}, i})
  - The weights \alpha (relative reliability of the fundamental model) and \beta
    (relative reliability of the public) are fit by maximum likelihood on
    historical Australian races:
    \alpha = 0.72 \quad \text{and} \quad \beta = 0.88
  - c_i is the resulting combined probability vector used for final wagering
    selection.

8.3 Goodness-of-Fit Metric: Pseudo-R^2 (\eta^2)

Evaluate the genuine, non-public informational value added by your combined
model relative to a baseline:

\eta^2(\text{model}) = 1 - \frac{LL(\text{Model})}{LL(\text{Random})}

\Delta\eta^2 = \eta^2(\text{combined model}) - \eta^2(\text{public odds alone})

Where LL(\text{Model}) is the log-likelihood of the probability model:

LL = \sum_{r=1}^{M} \ln(P(\text{winner}_r))

Programmatic Mandate: If \Delta\eta^2 \ge 0.05, the model contains significant,
unique non-public informational value. The race is classified as Edge Class A
(Low Entropy, High Conversion). If \Delta\eta^2 < 0.01, the model has failed to
extract an edge over the public; downgrade the race to Edge Class E and abstain
from Win/Place staking.

8.4 Pricing Exotic Bets: Corrected Harville Formula

For bets requiring the prediction of finishing order of \ge 2 horses (Quinella,
Trifecta, First 4), apply the corrected Harville formula by raising the combined
probabilities to two separate estimated exponents to adjust for longshot
underestimation and favorite overestimation:

1. Second-Place Corrected Probability:

P(\text{2nd} = k \mid \text{1st} = i) = \frac{c_k^{\gamma}}{\sum_{j \neq i} c_j^{\gamma}} \quad \text{where } \gamma = 0.81

2. Third-Place Corrected Probability:

P(\text{3rd} = m \mid \text{1st} = i, \text{2nd} = k) = \frac{c_m^{\delta}}{\sum_{j \neq i, k} c_j^{\delta}} \quad \text{where } \delta = 0.65
